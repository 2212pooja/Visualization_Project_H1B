Related Research

Previous research and analyses of H1B exist to demonstrate factors like approval rate with current immigration policy; H1B and Data Science field; Data Analyst vs Data Scientist. I used EDA processes to explore to view the outline of the dataset and other information needed to complete the research. I developed this project and deduced the current trends on total petitions in recent years and provide the graphical representation of data showing indicators of H1B submission by state, the type of jobs that attract new visa applications, employers who submit the greatest number of applications, states, and cities with the highest median salaries and a comparison of different years' data.
